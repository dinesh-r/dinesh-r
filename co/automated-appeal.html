Formal Appeal

Providers are required to participate in an Informal Reconsideration meeting to have Formal Appeal rights. Providers will receive a Notice of Adverse Action following the Informal Reconsideration meeting. A Notice of Adverse Action is sent within 60 days following the Informal Reconsideration meeting. Provider must submit a Formal Appeal through the Office of Administrative Courts within 30 days of receiving the Notice of Adverse Action.

Formal appeals are handled through the Office of Administrative Courts, established by the State of Colorado.
