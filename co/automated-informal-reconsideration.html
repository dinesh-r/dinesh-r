Informal Reconsideration

When HMS conducts a Complex or Automated Audit and issues a Notice of Informal Reconsideration, a provider may participate in the Informal Reconsideration process if they dispute any of the preliminary findings. The Informal Reconsideration process gives providers the opportunity to present their reasoning and any documentation supporting their dispute. Representatives from the provider, HCPF and HMS will participate in an Informal Reconsideration Meeting in good faith in an effort to resolve the dispute.

NOTE: Providers must participate in the Informal Reconsideration process before they can pursue a formal appeal. For more information on the Informal Reconsideration process, refer to section 25.5-4-301(3.3)(l)(IV) of the Revised Statutes (C.R.S.).

How to participate in the Informal Reconsideration process

Within sixty (60) days of receiving the Notice of Informal Reconsideration, providers must clearly identify which preliminary findings they plan to dispute and submit the reasoning and supporting documentation for those disputed findings through an Informal Reconsideration Request Form. Providers can access the Informal Reconsideration Request Form via the HMS Provider Portal or by contacting Provider Services.

Access Provider Portal

To access the provider portal, visit https://hmsportal.hms.com

Register for the Portal

Use the same link and choose Register. On the next screen, select the appropriate box and complete the registration form.

Contact Provider Services

To contact HMS Provider Services, providers should use the following email inbox: CORAC@gainwelltechnologies.com

After receiving the information described above, and within ninety (90) calendar days of the Notice of Informal Reconsideration, HMS will schedule an Informal Reconsideration Meeting with representatives from the provider, HCPF and HMS at a mutually agreed date and time.

NOTE: If a provider does not participate in the Informal Reconsideration process, the provider will not be able to submit a Formal Appeal.
