Preliminary Finding

You will receive a notification of preliminary findings once HMS and HCPF determine there is a potential overpayment. This is not an overpayment determination or notice of adverse action, and there has been no discovery of an overpayment pursuant to 42 C.F.R. ยง 433.316. These are preliminary findings of a potential overpayment only.

This notice will include the dates of service that were included in the audit. Providers will have 30 days from the date they receive the Notice of Preliminary Findings to do the following:

Automated Audit

Identify claims in the HMS Provider Portal that they disagree with and would like to discuss in an Informal Reconsideration Meeting. An Informal Reconsideration is required for providers to have Formal Appeal Rights.

Complex Audit

  • Identify claims in the HMS Provider Portal that they disagree with and would like to discuss during an Exit conference. Exit Conferences are optional and not required for providers to move to an Informal Reconsideration or Formal Appeal.
  • Identify claims in the HMS Provider Portal that they disagree with and would like to discuss in an Informal Reconsideration Meeting. An Informal Reconsideration is required for providers to have Formal Appeal Rights.
