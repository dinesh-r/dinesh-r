Medical Records Request

You will receive a notification letter

  • If your facility is chosen for a review, a medical record request letter will be mailed to the medical records HCPF informing them of the upcoming review.
  • If the medical record is not received in the requested time, HMS will make courtesy calls and mail follow-up letters.

Instructions are included

  • The letter will include instructions for submitting the medical records, the list of claims to be reviewed, and the number of days in which you must submit information.
    • Providers have forty-five (45) days to submit medical records to HMS.
      • Providers can request an extension by contacting HMS Provider Services.
  • The preferred method of sending medical record files is via the HMS Provider Portal or SFTP.
  • For additional options for submitting medical records, providers may contact 877-401-3635. This number will also be included in your medical record request letter.

HMS protects your data including PHI

HMS protects data provided by providers using the highest security standards in the industry.

Address Updates

Your initial Medical Record Request letter will be mailed to the address on file. If you would like to have medical record requests or any follow-up letter mailed to a different address you have two choices.

  • Provide updated address/contact information to the provider service representative and they will update the address and contact information in our system.
  • Providers can log into the HMS Provider Portal and update the address and contact information themselves at anytime.
