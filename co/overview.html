Overview

The Colorado RAC Program is a federally mandated initiative under the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act, designed to identify and recover improper Medicaid payments. HMS has served as Colorado’s RAC vendor since 2021, partnering with the Colorado HCPF of Health Care Policy and Financing (HCPF), to ensure program integrity and financial stewardship.

Scope of Work

Complex Reviews

Targeted audits requiring medical record review, conducted by clinicians and coders.

Automated Reviews

Data-driven audits that identify improper payments without requiring medical records.

Population Covered

Currently focused on Fee-for-Service (FFS) Medicaid claims.
